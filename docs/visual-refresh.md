# KeepClean Visual Refresh

## Goals

- Keep the app extremely clear and safe for a stressful utility action.
- Make the interface feel brighter, calmer, and more premium.
- Introduce a memorable brand mark that works both in the window and as an app icon.
- Keep all styling lightweight and native-feeling on macOS.

## Brand Direction

- Use a simple rounded keycap as the base form.
- Add a soft cleaning sweep and a small sparkle accent to make the mark recognizable.
- Keep the palette warm and polished: cream, deep graphite, electric blue, and amber.
- Favor generous spacing, layered cards, and subtle materials over heavy decoration.

## UI Direction

- Turn the `Clean` tab into a hero-style control surface with clearer hierarchy.
- Keep the two cleaning actions large and obvious, but give them distinct visual personalities.
- Use compact status and safety cards so errors, countdowns, and recovery states feel intentional.
- Refresh `Settings` and `About` so they match the same visual system instead of reading like default forms.

## Constraints

- Do not weaken any safety messaging.
- Do not add networking, background work, or heavy animation.
- Keep the app responsive and simple enough to maintain as a small Swift project.
